Computer Science 50 - AP

During my Sophomore year at Kennett High School, I took CS50 AP. Although I passed the AP exam, the course itself was not completely AP yet, which is why I took the course again my Senior year. CS50 was my first official Computer Science course, so it's where I got to learn the basics.

CS50 AP started in C, which at one point was my favorite programming language (trust me, I've moved on). This course has a collection of small problems to be solved, all of which could be checked, using Check50, before being submitted, using Submit50. Through CS50 AP, I learned about variables, loops, and functions.
